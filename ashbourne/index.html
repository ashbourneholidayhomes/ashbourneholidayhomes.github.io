Ashbourne

Ashbourne is a small picturesque market town in the Derbyshire Dales. Ashbourne is a great destination for couples, families and groups as it has something for every individual.

Landmarks

Asbourne is at the start of the Tissington Trail, which follows the course of the Ashbourne to Buxton railway. It brings you up through the countyside and if you make it all the way to tissington, get yourself an icecream.

There are many historical artifacts in town such as the famous and rare 'gallows' sign across St John's Street. This can be seen in the medieval street pattern that runs throughout the town such as the cobbled market place.

There are many stately homes in the area surrounding Ashbourne with Chatsworth house, Sudbry Hall, Hardwick Hall and Tissington Hall.
